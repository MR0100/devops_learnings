# L29/C06/T03 — Competing Offers

## Learning Objectives

- Use competing offers
- Maximize comp

## Why

Competing offers = leverage:
- "Match X" works
- Recruiter goes to bat
- Real lift

For: most valuable.

## Get Multiple

- Apply broadly
- Time interviews
- 2-3 offers ideal
- Same week if possible

## Disclose Carefully

```
"I have another offer at $X. Can you match or beat?"
```

Or:
```
"I'm considering offer at $X. What can we do?"
```

## Don't Lie

Companies share info sometimes.
Lies caught: offer rescinded.

For: truthful.

## Strategic Sharing

- Total comp: yes
- Company: sometimes
- Specifics: depends

For: judgment.

## Counter-Offer Process

1. Get offer 1
2. Use to leverage current process
3. Or accept and notify
4. Counter offers come

## Time Pressure

Recruiters push for fast decision:
- "Need answer by Friday"

Push back:
- "Need 1 more week"
- Usually given

## Negotiation Conversation

```
"Thanks for the offer. I'm excited.
I have offer at $X from Company Y.
Can you match or improve?"
```

Recruiter:
- Goes to hiring manager
- May come back with bump

## What Improves

- Sign-on (most flexible)
- RSU (sometimes)
- Base (band-capped)
- Level (rare)

## Walking Power

If can walk:
- Stronger position
- Better counter

If must take:
- Weaker
- Accept what offered

For: have alternatives.

## Best Practices

- Multiple offers
- Honest leverage
- Patient
- Walking power

## Common Mistakes

- One offer (no leverage)
- Lie about other offers
- Accept first
- Reveal current comp early

## Quick Refs

```
Goal: 2-3 competing offers
Use: total comp leverage
Honest
Push back on time pressure
```
