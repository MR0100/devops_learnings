# L29/C06/T04 — levels.fyi & TeamBlind Realism

## Learning Objectives

- Use data
- Realistic expectations

## levels.fyi

Crowd-sourced comp data:
- Per company
- Per level
- Per location
- Per year

For: bands.

## TeamBlind

Anonymous tech forum:
- Negotiation tips
- Real numbers
- War stories

For: insider info.

## Use Data

Before negotiating:
- Look up bands
- Median + 75th %ile
- Recent grants

For: realistic ask.

## Limitations

- Self-reported (skew)
- Recent grants (RSU value varies)
- Location-specific
- Anti-stale (check dates)

## Inflation

Recent years:
- Comp grew massively
- Then stabilized
- Variable by company

## Refresh Rates

- RSU values shift with stock
- Negotiate at current valuation
- Watch market

## Geographic

US Coastal:
- SF Bay, NY: highest
- Seattle: high
- Austin / Denver: mid-high
- Remote: discount sometimes

International:
- London: 60-70% of SF
- Tel Aviv: high
- India: lower (PPP-adjusted)
- Etc.

## Negotiation Realistic

Don't:
- Demand top of band without justification
- Show no data
- Refuse moderate offer

Do:
- Ask for 90th percentile if signal strong
- Backed by data
- Reasonable counter

## Best Practices

- Research before
- Recent data
- Geographic adjustment
- Median + stretch

## Common Mistakes

- No data
- Stale data
- Demand unrealistic
- Settle low
