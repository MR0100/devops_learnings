# L29/C03/T02 — Drive the Discussion

## Learning Objectives

- Lead system design
- Show seniority

## Why Drive

Interviewer is signal:
- Senior+: drive
- Junior: react

For: show level.

## How

- Propose direction
- Justify
- Move forward
- Don't wait

## Lead Statements

- "I'll start by clarifying..."
- "Now I'd like to estimate..."
- "Let me draw the high-level..."
- "Should I dive into X or cover Y first?"

For: show structure.

## Manage Time

- "I'll spend 10 min on deep dive"
- "Want to spend rest on scale"

For: pacing.

## Show Options

For each decision:
- A vs B trade-off
- Why pick A
- When B better

For: judgment.

## Avoid

- "What should I do next?"
- Pause without action
- Wait for hints

For: interviewer worried.

## Engage

- "Does this approach make sense?"
- "Should I cover X?"
- "Any concerns?"

For: dialogue.

## Adapt

If interviewer:
- Pushes one area: dive
- Hints at issue: address
- Asks question: answer

For: flexible.

## Confidence

- Speak with conviction
- Justify choices
- OK to be wrong; defend

For: senior signal.

## Drive Trade-Offs

Always:
- Multiple options considered
- Pick + justify
- Acknowledge downsides

For: maturity.

## Best Practices

- Drive, don't react
- Justify decisions
- Manage time
- Adapt to feedback

## Common Mistakes

- Passive
- Wait for prompt
- One option no trade-off
- No justification

## Quick Refs

```
Drive: lead the discussion
Justify: every choice
Time: manage
Adapt: to feedback
```

## Interview Prep

**Senior**: "Drive design."

**Staff**: "Show seniority."
