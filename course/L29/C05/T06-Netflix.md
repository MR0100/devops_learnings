# L29/C05/T06 — Netflix (Keeper Test, Freedom & Responsibility)

## Learning Objectives

- Prep Netflix
- Show senior-level

## Roles

- Senior SWE
- Sr Staff
- Principal

Netflix culture: senior-only hiring.

## Levels

Flat:
- Senior
- Sr Staff
- Principal

For: high bar.

## Process

1. Recruiter
2. Phone screens (multiple)
3. Onsite (intense):
   - Coding (1-2)
   - System design (1-2)
   - Behavioral (1-2)
   - Many interviewers

## Culture

- Freedom + Responsibility
- High performance
- "Keeper Test"
- Context not control

## Keeper Test

"Would I fight to keep this person?"

If no: severance.

For: high bar; high pay.

## Compensation

Famously high:
- All cash (not RSU)
- Higher than FAANG often

For: top talent.

## Coding

- Medium-Hard
- Practical

## System Design

- Senior+
- Complex
- Real-world

## Behavioral

- Senior-level only
- "Tell me a senior story"
- No mid-level stories

## Specific

- Show senior judgment
- Mature decision-making
- Big-picture

## Best Practices

- Senior+ stories only
- Strong technical
- Cultural fit (responsibility)

## Common Mistakes

- Junior-feeling stories
- Weak design
- Apply if not senior+

## Quick Refs

```
Senior+ only
Cash heavy
Cultural fit (responsibility)
Strong design
```
