# L29/C05/T01 — Amazon (LP-Heavy, Frugality, Ownership)

## Learning Objectives

- Prep Amazon-specific
- Win bar raiser

## Roles

- SDE (Software Development Engineer)
- SRE (Site Reliability Engineer)
- TPM (Technical Program Manager)

## DevOps at Amazon

SDE with DevOps focus or pure SRE.

## Levels

- L4: SDE I
- L5: SDE II (mid-senior)
- L6: Senior SDE
- L7: Principal SDE

## Process

1. Recruiter
2. Phone screen (coding)
3. Onsite (5 rounds):
   - Coding (1-2)
   - System design (1-2; senior+)
   - Behavioral (heavy LP focus)
   - Bar Raiser (1, mixed)

## LP Focus

Every round: LP-focused questions.

For: prep stories per LP.

## Top LPs DevOps

- Ownership
- Bias for Action
- Dive Deep
- Insist on Highest Standards
- Earn Trust
- Right, A Lot

## Bar Raiser

External:
- Veto power
- Strict
- Behavioral-heavy

For: extra LP prep.

## Frugality

Amazon culture:
- Cost-conscious
- Examples valued:
  - "Reduced bill $500k via..."
  - "Built solution without buying tool..."

## Customer Obsession

Even for internal:
- Customer = your service users (devs)
- Or external customers

## Coding

- Medium LeetCode
- Sometimes harder
- Edge cases important

## System Design

For L5+:
- Single-region
- Single service or two
- Cover scale + reliability

For L6+:
- Multi-region
- Complex
- Trade-offs

## Compensation

- Base capped (~$200k)
- Heavy RSUs (4-year, back-loaded 5/15/40/40)
- Sign-on (year 1 + 2)
- Year 1 cliff

For: model carefully.

## Best Practices

- Stories per LP (2-3 each)
- Frugality examples
- Customer obsession
- Practice Bar Raiser questions

## Common Mistakes

- Generic stories
- No frugality story
- Weak Bar Raiser prep
