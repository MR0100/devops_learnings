# L29/C01/T03 — Onsite Loop

## Learning Objectives

- Survive 5-hour interview
- Pace yourself

## Format

Typically 4-6 rounds, 45-60 min each:
- 1-2 coding
- 1-2 system design
- 1-2 behavioral
- 1 hiring manager
- Plus lunch / hallway

## Coding Rounds

For DevOps:
- Algorithms (med)
- Or systems (rate limiter, log parser)

(See L29/C02.)

## System Design Rounds

- Senior+ heavy
- 45 min design
- Bar-raising

(See L29/C03.)

## Behavioral

- "Tell me about a time..."
- STAR format
- Per company values

(See L29/C04.)

## Hiring Manager

- Team fit
- Project alignment
- Q&A

## Bar Raiser (Amazon)

External:
- Set bar
- Veto power

(See L29/C01/T04.)

## Pacing

5-hour interview:
- Stay sharp
- Hydrate
- Snack
- Pee break

## Mental Prep

- Sleep
- Don't cram
- Trust prep
- Confident

## Between Rounds

- Reset
- Don't dwell
- Brief recovery

## Strong Rounds

Bar:
- Coding: clean, optimal, edge cases
- System: scale, trade-offs
- Behavioral: STAR, specific, growth

## Recover from Bad

If one round bad:
- Don't carry over
- Strong other rounds
- Hiring committee weights all

## Notes

- Take after each
- Prep for next based on feel

## Best Practices

- Sleep before
- Practice 5-round day
- Don't dwell
- Strong finish

## Common Mistakes

- Fatigue late
- Carry mood between
- No questions
- Underprepared one round

## Quick Refs

```
Format: 4-6 × 45-60 min
Mix: coding + design + behavioral
Pace: maintain energy
```
