# L29/C01/T01 — Recruiter Screen

## Learning Objectives

- Pass recruiter screen
- Set up well

## Recruiter Call

First step (15-30 min):
- Resume walk
- Why this company
- Compensation expectation
- Logistics

## Resume Walk

```
"Tell me about your background."
```

Prepare 60-90 sec elevator pitch:
- Current role + impact
- 1-2 highlights
- What you want next

## Why This Company

Show interest:
- Specific reasons (mission, tech, scale)
- What attracts you
- NOT: "any FAANGM"

## Comp Expectations

Recruiter will ask:
- "What's your expectation?"

Best answer:
- "Looking at total package... in range $X-Y"
- Or: "I'd like to learn more before discussing"
- Or: "Help me understand the band for this level"

Don't:
- Low-ball
- Be specific too early

## Logistics

- Timeline
- Interview format (virtual / onsite)
- Reschedule policies
- Process steps

## Show Knowledge

About company:
- Recent news
- Product knowledge
- Tech stack (educated guess)

## Questions

Ask:
- "Team focus?"
- "Tech stack?"
- "Interview rubric?"
- "Timeline?"

## Logistics Done Right

- Confirmed slots
- Clear next steps
- Recruiter as ally

For: smooth process.

## Red Flags

- Vague timeline
- Pushy on comp early
- Won't share format

If multiple: reconsider.

## Best Practices

- Research company
- Prep pitch
- Range for comp
- Ask questions
- Follow up

## Common Mistakes

- Underprepared
- Reveal current comp
- No questions
- Generic interest
