# L01/C06 — Common Misconceptions & Anti-Patterns

## Chapter Overview

You will inherit teams that "do DevOps" but get nothing from it. The most important skill at staff level is recognizing these failure modes quickly. This chapter is a field guide.

## Learning Outcomes

- Identify each anti-pattern from a 30-second conversation
- Articulate the root cause and recommended intervention for each
- Avoid creating them in your own org

## Top-10 Anti-Patterns at a Glance

1. The "DevOps Team" silo
2. Tools-first ("we bought Kubernetes")
3. NoOps fantasy
4. Maturity model checkbox
5. DORA gaming
6. Postmortem theater (blameless on paper)
7. Platform team without product manager
8. Centralized infra team becomes ticket queue
9. Premature microservices
10. Per-engineer metrics
