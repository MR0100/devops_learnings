# L29/C04/T06 — Crafting a Career Story Bank

## Learning Objectives

- Build story bank
- Use for any question

## Story Bank

12+ stories ready:
- STAR format
- Tagged
- Practiced

## Categories

For each LP / pillar:
- 1-2 stories

For common themes:
- 1-2 stories

## Bank Template

```markdown
## Story: Reduced Payments MTTR

### Tags
- Ownership
- Bias for Action
- Earn Trust
- Deliver Results

### S
Payments incidents averaged 30 min MTTR. SLO at risk.

### T
Owned: bring MTTR < 10 min in Q1.

### A
- Audited last 50 incidents
- Identified top 3 noise sources
- Built better alerts (SLO-based)
- Wrote runbooks for top 5
- Trained team
- Drilled monthly

### R
- MTTR: 30 → 7 min
- Reduced incident count 40%
- Team confidence up

### Lessons
- Data-driven prioritization
- Training matters more than tooling

### Follow-up Prep
Q: "What would you do differently?"
A: Earlier involvement of on-call rotation.
```

## Maintain

- Update with new stories
- Refresh details
- Practice periodically

## Tag for Companies

- Amazon: LPs
- Google: Googleyness
- Meta: pillars
- Custom: any

## Practice

- Out loud
- Time
- Friend / coach feedback

## Quantify

Numbers wherever possible:
- "$200k saved"
- "40% faster"
- "10 to 100 deploys/day"

## Best Practices

- 12+ stories
- Tagged
- Practiced
- Updated

## Common Mistakes

- 1-2 stories (recycled)
- Not practiced
- No numbers
- Stale

## Quick Refs

```

```
