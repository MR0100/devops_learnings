# L29/C04/T05 — Common Themes

## Learning Objectives

- Cover all themes
- Story per

## Common Themes

- Ambiguity
- Conflict
- Influence (no authority)
- Failure
- Trade-offs
- Mentorship
- Cross-functional
- Customer impact
- Strategic vision
- Recovery from setback

## Story Per Theme

Have 1-2 each.

## Conflict

Q: "Disagreed with someone."

Frame:
- Specific person + topic
- Your perspective + theirs
- Resolved via data/conversation
- Outcome
- Relationship preserved

## Influence

Q: "Got buy-in for change."

Frame:
- What you proposed
- Why hesitation
- How you convinced
- Outcome

## Failure

Q: "Tell me about a failure."

Frame:
- Project failed
- Your role
- What happened
- What you learned
- How you grew

NOT:
- Blame
- Trivial
- "No real failures"

## Trade-Offs

Q: "Hard decision."

Frame:
- Two options
- Trade-off
- Your decision
- Why
- Outcome

## Ambiguity

Q: "Vague project."

Frame:
- No clear spec
- How you scoped
- Stakeholder alignment
- Drove forward
- Result

## Cross-Functional

Q: "Worked with non-engineering."

Frame:
- Product / sales / etc.
- Communication
- Aligned
- Result

## Best Practices

- Multiple stories per theme
- Specific
- Self-aware
- Growth mindset

## Common Mistakes

- Vague
- No reflection
- Blame others
- Trivial failure
