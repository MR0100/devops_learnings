# L29/C04/T03 — Google's Googleyness & Leadership

## Learning Objectives

- Pass Google behavioral
- Show Googleyness

## Googleyness

Soft skills:
- Comfort with ambiguity
- Bias for action
- Collaboration
- Conscientious / responsible
- Striving for excellence

## Leadership

Even IC:
- Lead by influence
- Mentor
- Drive projects
- Cross-team

## Format

Behavioral round:
- 1-2 questions
- 45 minutes

## Common Questions

- Tell me about an ambiguous project.
- A time you disagreed.
- A time you taught someone.
- Cross-team collaboration.
- Failure + what you learned.

## Prep

Stories like Amazon:
- STAR
- 10-15 stories
- Tagged by trait

## Specific to Google

### Ambiguity
- "Project had no clear spec; I..."

### Collaboration
- "Worked with 3 teams; I..."

### Excellence
- "Pushed quality bar; result was..."

### Conscientious
- "Took responsibility for X..."

### Action
- "Took initiative without being asked..."

## Tone

- Humble
- Growth mindset
- Team-oriented
- Curious

## Common Mistakes

- Self-promoting
- Negative about others
- Vague
- No "we" balance

## Best Practices

- Specific stories
- Show learning
- Acknowledge team
- Quantify
