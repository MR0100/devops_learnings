# L01/C04 — Roles, Titles & Career Paths

## Chapter Overview

The job market is full of overlapping titles: DevOps Engineer, SRE, Platform Engineer, Cloud Engineer, Infrastructure Engineer, Production Engineer, Reliability Engineer. This chapter cuts through the noise so you can target roles correctly and progress your career deliberately.

## Learning Outcomes

- Match job descriptions to the actual day-to-day work
- Choose roles that match your strengths and career goals
- Understand the FAANGM leveling ladder
- Build a 12–24-month plan to the next level

## Title Reality Check

> *"DevOps Engineer", "SRE", and "Platform Engineer" are 70% the same work and 30% different emphasis. The difference matters most at senior levels.*
