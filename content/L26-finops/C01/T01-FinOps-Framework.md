# L26/C01/T01 — The FinOps Foundation Framework

## Learning Objectives

- Understand FinOps
- Apply principles

## FinOps

Cloud financial management:
- Cross-functional
- Engineering + finance + business
- Cost visibility + accountability

## Foundation

FinOps Foundation (Linux Foundation):
- Standards
- Certifications
- Best practices

## Principles

1. Teams need to collaborate
2. Decisions driven by business value
3. Everyone owns cloud usage
4. FinOps reports accessible + timely
5. A centralized team drives FinOps
6. Take advantage of variable cost model

## Phases

### Inform
Visibility:
- Where is spend?
- Who owns?
- Tagging, dashboards

### Optimize
Action:
- Right-size
- Reserved / Spot
- Eliminate waste

### Operate
Run:
- Continuous
- Automation
- Culture

### Crawl
- Tagging

### Walk
- Optimization

### Run

## Outcomes

- Reduce waste
- Better commits (RI/SP)
- Right-size
- Cost-aware architecture

## Best Practices

- Cross-functional team
- Regular review
- Tagging strict
- Automation

## Common Mistakes

- Finance-only (no eng buy-in)
- One-time effort
- No tagging
- No accountability
