# L26/C07/T03 — Engineering Incentives

## Learning Objectives

- Build cost culture
- Incentivize savings

## Culture

Cost-aware engineering:
- Designs consider cost
- Reviews include cost
- Wins celebrated

## Approaches

### Make Visible
Dashboards everywhere.

### Per-Team Showback
Team sees cost.

### Cost in Reviews
PR template includes cost impact.

### Cost in Design Docs
Architecture reviews discuss cost.

### Goals / OKRs
Team KPIs include cost.

### Celebrate Wins
"Team A saved $50k/mo."

## Avoid

### Punishment
Don't punish for high cost.
- Bad design culture
- Hidden costs
- Misallocate

### Pure Top-Down
Engineers don't engage.

## Education

Train:
- Cloud cost models
- Common mistakes
- Tools

For: enable.

## Tools for Engineers

Self-service:
- Dashboards
- Right-sizing tools
- Forecasting

For: empower.

## Rewards

- Public recognition
- Performance review credit
- Bonus tied (carefully)

## Cost Champions

Per team:
- FinOps champion
- Coordinates
- Promotes

## Bridge Eng + Finance

- Joint meetings
- Shared metrics
- Common language

For: collaboration.

## Cost Reviews

Quarterly:
- Per team
- Trends
- Opportunities
- Action items

## Architecture Cost

In design:
- Estimate
- Justify
- Optimize

For: build in cost-aware.

## Best Practices

- Visible (dashboards)
- Showback
- Education
- Champions
- Celebrate wins
- Long-term focus

## Common Mistakes

- Punish
- Top-down only
- No tooling
- One-time push

## Real Examples

### Stripe
Strong cost culture.

### Netflix
Engineers own cost.

### Many growth-stage
Implementing.
